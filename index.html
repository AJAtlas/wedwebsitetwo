Wilderness Environment Design

Designing immersive environments for your next project.

Wilderness is a UK-based environment design studio specialising in voxel landscapes and 3D environments. Since 2015, our products have been used in video production, educational material, gaming, and more.

01 — About

Studio Overview

Founded in 2015, Wilderness has delivered over 200 commercial projects to clients across the globe.

We specialise in atmospheric voxel terrain that makes you feel that you are in the scene.

Our work excels in environmental storytelling to make the world itself become a character.
