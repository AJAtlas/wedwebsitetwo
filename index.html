Wilderness is a UK-based studio specialising in voxel landscapes, cinematic environments and worldbuilding. We work with studios, agencies and creators to produce meaningful digital worlds.

About

Founded in 2015, Wilderness has delivered over 200 commercial projects across games, film and digital media. Our work focuses on atmosphere, realism and narrative-driven design.

We prioritise clarity of communication, structured workflows and long-term collaboration with our partners.

Approach

Our process combines technical expertise with artistic direction. Each project begins with research and concept development, followed by structured production and iterative refinement.

We maintain close communication with our clients throughout, ensuring each environment aligns with both creative vision and technical requirements.
